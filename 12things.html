12 THINGS PEOPLE CAN’T LET GO OF

2026

Adobe Illustrator

Inspired by the Seoul 1988 Olympic icons, I created 12 new icons representing things people can’t let go of. Through my icons I represent physical attachments, relational issues, and what we hold onto within ourselves.

1. A little bit of money

2. Food, food, and food

3. Obsessing over your looks

4. An addiction that ruins your life

5. Old friendships that ended

6. Secrets a close friend told you

7. Resentment towards someone who wronged you

8. One-sided love

9. A fantasy you want to live

10. Guilt from something you’ve done

11. Jealousy against someone else

12. Hope
